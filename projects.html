Physics Tryouts

Genre: 3D, Racing simulator, Tank simulator, Physics based, Low Poly

Engine: Unity Engine, C#


  • Developed player movement without the use of rigidbody or built in unity physics, all physics related movement was done with code inside their respective scripts.

  • Developed a drag and velocity system using newtons second law within scripts to either increase velocity over time. the drag system uses the weight of the tank to slow down over time. Most of the calculations were done with code and used very little Unity components.

  • Tank Turret moves on it's own compared to the rest of the vehicle which was designed in a way for the player to be able to move in one direction but can look in another.

  • Tank wheels rotate depending on how fast you move and same thing when you move side to side.

  • While the tank turret is uses more for side to side movement with your mouse, the tank barrel was developed for looking up and down and like that the tank can fire in any direction that player aims at.

  • Tank missle is also using physics and gravity which was designed in scripts, falling over time based on the weight and the velocity that it was fired from.

  • Developed and designed the level with the knowledge that it was going to have 3 different layers, the first layer is the race track where the tank can move at normal speeds i.e the drag would just be set to normal. The second layer is the grass layer which significantly slows down the tank if it ended up on the grass i.e the drag would increase to slow the tank down more. the third layer is the speed track layer which reduces the drag of tank to allow it to propel itself forward with a minor speed boost, while reducing drag.

  • A small UI manager was developed for retcile placement that the tank barrel would try to fire at.
